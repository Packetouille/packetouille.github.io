Interests

I am an aspiring network engineer, and in my spare time, I run a little online retail business where I create movie displays using authentic 35mm film. Working in the film exhibition industry for twenty years has given me the opportunity to collect a lot of film. A few years ago I decided to open up the vault and share my collection with the world out of the goodness of my heart, and it is in no way related to me annoying my wife with a cluttered basement for years. I'm proud to say that, to date, I've shipped displays to 36 states in the US and to 6 other countries including Canada, France, Switzerland, Italy, the United Arab Emirates, and New Zealand.
